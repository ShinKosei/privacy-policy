Privacy Policy – JLPT Dojo

Last updated: 30 December 2025

JLPT Dojo (“the App”) is a language learning application designed to help users study Japanese (JLPT N5 level). Your privacy is important to us.

1. Overview

JLPT Dojo does not require account creation and does not collect personally identifiable information. All learning progress is stored locally on your device.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Account credentials
  • Location data
  • Photos, media, or files
  • Device identifiers used for tracking
  • Usage analytics linked to individuals

3. Local Data Storage

The app stores learning progress, XP, streaks, statistics, and preferences locally on the user’s device only. This data never leaves the device.

4. Text-to-Speech (TTS)

The app uses the device’s built-in text-to-speech engine. No audio or spoken data is recorded or transmitted.

5. Third-Party Services

JLPT Dojo currently uses no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Children’s Privacy

No personal data is collected from children or adults.

7. Data Security

All data is stored locally on the user’s device.

8. Your Rights

Because no personal data is collected, uninstalling the app removes all stored data automatically.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the app evolves.
